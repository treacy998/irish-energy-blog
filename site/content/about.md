## What This Is

A daily, data-driven blog covering Ireland's wholesale electricity markets. Every chart is generated from real SEMO and EirGrid data using Python. Every post aims to make the I-SEM more transparent and understandable.

## Who We Are

**Eoin** — Energy broker and founder of INIS Energy, a brokerage based in Cork. Background in software development with a focus on the intersection of energy markets and data analysis. Building expertise in wholesale market mechanics, flexibility services, and grid operations.

## The Pipeline

All analysis is produced using Python (pandas, matplotlib).

## Data Sources

- **SEMO** — Day-Ahead Market price results
- **EirGrid** — Generation mix, wind forecasts, system demand, SNSP
- **CRU** — Regulatory decisions and consultation documents
